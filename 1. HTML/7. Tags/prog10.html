Space Discovery

Recent space discoveries include the detection of free-floating rogue planets, a glimpse of a solar system being born, and the discovery of a mysterious object dancing with Neptune. Scientists are also actively exploring the potential for life on other planets and investigating the mysteries of black holes and dark matter.

Film City

There are two prominent "Film Cities" in India: Ramoji Film City in Hyderabad and Film City in Mumbai (also known as Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari). Ramoji Film City is the world's largest integrated film studio complex, spanning 2500 acres. Mumbai Film City, located in Goregaon, is a major hub for Bollywood filmmaking.
